Banana Bread

Description

Banana bread is a type of quick bread made with mashed bananas. It is often sweetened with sugar and can include nuts, chocolate chips, or spices like cinnamon. It is a popular way to use overripe bananas.

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a mixing bowl, mash the ripe bananas with a fork until smooth.
  3. Add the melted butter and mix well.
  4. Stir in the baking soda and salt.
  5. Add the sugar, beaten egg, and vanilla extract. Mix until combined.
  6. Gradually add the flour and mix until just combined. Do not overmix.
  7. If desired, fold in nuts or chocolate chips.
  8. Pour the batter into a greased loaf pan.
  9. Bake for 60-70 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  10. Let it cool in the pan for 10 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.
  11. Slice and serve warm or at room temperature.
  12. Store leftovers in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days or refrigerate for up to a week.
  13. For longer storage, wrap the banana bread tightly in plastic wrap and freeze for up to 3 months.
  14. Thaw frozen banana bread in the refrigerator overnight before serving.
  15. Enjoy your delicious banana bread!
